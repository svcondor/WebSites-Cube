How to use the app

Drag along a side to rotate a cube face.
Drag along center to rotate the whole cube in all 3 directions.

Reset – to reset the cube
Scramble – to scramble the cube
Undo – undo the last move
Slow/Fast – change cube rotation speed

How to solve the cube

Notation Used in solving instructions

F rotate the front face clockwise
F' rotate the front face anti-clockwise
U U' rotate the upper top face
D D' rotate the lower down face
R R' rotate the right face
L L' rotate the left face
B B' rotate the back face

Explain edge pieces and corner pieces?

Step 1 - White Cross

Rotate the whole cube so that the white center piece is on the UP face
Make a white cross on the UP face
The side colors of the white cross pieces must correpond to the center pieces below them.

Step 3 - Edge pieces of the middle layer


Flip the cube so that the yellow center piece is on the UP face
Rotate the cube and/or the UP face so that an edge piece has the same color as the center piece on the front face below it. The top colour of the piece must not be yellow.

U' L' U L U F U' F' to rotate that piece to the left
U R U' R' U' F' U F to rotate that piece to the right

If there are no suitable pieces on the top layer then do one of the above moves to get a piece from the middle layer to the top.

Step 4 - Make a Yellow cross on the top layer

Don't worry about the side colors of the yellow cross yet.
Rotate the top layer to get 3 yellow in a row horizontally, then Do.
FRUR'U'F'
Else rotate the top layer to get yellow 'L' in far left, then Do.
FURU'R'F'
else
FURU'R'F'
Repeat until you get a yellow cross

Step 5 - Correct the side colors of the Yellow cross

To swap the front yellow tile with the left yellow tile, do the following.
RUR'URUUR'U

Step 6 - Get the yellow corners to their correct positions

Don't worry about their orientation yet i.e. if the yellow tile is on top. If the 4 corner tiles are in their correct positions go to step 7.

If one of the tiles is in the correct position whether or not the yellow tile is on top then rotate the whole cube until that piece is at the top left of the front face. Do the following move: URU'L'UR'U'L
Repeat the above up to 3 times to get all the yellow corners in their correct locations.

Step 7 - Orientate the yellow corners correctly

Throughout this step, keep the yellow center tile on top and the blue center tile facing you as the front face. During this step some of the lower pieces will get scrambled, follow the procedure and they will be back in place by the end. Keep the blue center tile facing you as the front face.

Rotate the UP face so that a piece without a yellow top tile is at the top right corner of the front face.
Do the following either 2 or 6 times until this top right tile is orientated correctly. Don't worry if some of the lower tiles are scambled.
R'D'R D

Without moving the blue front center rotate the top layer until another incorrectly orientated piece is in the top right position.
Do the same moves again and repeat for all the incorrect yellow corners.
rotate the top face to allign with the lower 2 faces and you're DONE!
