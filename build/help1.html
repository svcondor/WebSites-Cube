Cube - Help

How to use the app

Drag along a side to rotate a cube face.
Drag along center to rotate the whole cube in all 3 directions.

Reset – to reset the cube
Scramble – to scramble the cube
Elipsis - Bring up menu for Help, Crib, Sbout
Left arrow – undo the last move
Right Arrow - do the next step for me

How to solve the cube

Notation Used in solving instructions

F Front face - left on the display
B Back face - opposite the front
R Right face - right on the display
L Left face - opposite the right
U Upper face - top on the display
D Down face - opposite the upper

F rotate the front face clockwise
F' rotate the front face anti-clockwise
U U' rotate the upper top face
D D' rotate the lower down face
R R' rotate the right face
L L' rotate the left face
B B' rotate the back face

There are 8 corner pieces each with 3 color tiles
There are 12 side pieces each with 2 color tiles
The color of a face is the color of the center tile

Step 1 - White Cross

Rotate the whole cube so that the white center piece is on the UP face
Make a white cross on the UP face
The side colors of the white cross pieces must correpond to the center pieces below them.

Step 2 - white Corner pieces

Move the 4 white corner pieces to their correct location and orientation

Step 3 - Edge pieces of the middle layer

Rotate the whole cube so that the yellow center piece is on the UP face
Rotate the cube and/or the UP face so that an edge piece has the same color as the center piece on the FRONT face below it. The top colour of the piece must not be yellow.

U' L' U L U F U' F' to rotate that piece anticlockwise to the left
U R U' R' U' F' U F to rotate that piece clockwise to the right

If there are no suitable pieces on the UPPER layer then do one of the above moves to get a piece from the middle layer to the top.

Step 4 - Make a Yellow cross on the top layer

Don't worry about the side colors of the yellow cross yet.
Rotate the UP face to get 3 yellow in middle above FRONT face then
F R U R' U' F'
otherwise rotate the UP face to get yellow 'L' in back left, then Do.
F U R U' R' F'
otherwise
F U R U' R' F'
Repeat until you get a yellow cross

Step 5 - Correct the side colors of the Yellow cross

To swap the front yellow tile with the left yellow tile, do the following.
R U R' U R U U R' U
repeat if necessary

Step 6 - Get the yellow corners to their correct positions

Don't worry about their orientation yet i.e. if the yellow tile is on top. If the 4 corner tiles are in their correct positions go to step 7.

If one of the tiles is in the correct position whether or not the yellow tile is on top then rotate the whole cube until that piece is at the top left of the front face. Do the following move: U R U' L' U R' U' L
Repeat the above up to 3 times to get all the yellow corners in their correct locations.

Step 7 - Orientate the yellow corners correctly

Throughout this step, keep the yellow center tile on the UP face and the blue center tile facing you as the FRONT face. During this step some of the lower pieces will get scrambled, follow the procedure and they will be back in place when all 4 yellow corners are correct. Keep the blue center tile facing you as the front face.

Rotate the UP face so that a piece without a yellow top tile is at the top right corner of the front face.
Do the following either 2 or 6 times until this top right tile is orientated correctly. Some of the lower tiles might be scrambled until all four corners are correct scambled.
R' D' R D

Without moving the blue FRONT center rotate the UP face until another incorrectly orientated piece is in the top right position.
Do the same moves again and repeat for all the incorrect yellow corners.
rotate the UP face to allign with the lower 2 faces and you're DONE!
